TiNO Digital Design Project

Digital design project developing a visual prototype for the TiNO device. Currently still in development.

The Concept

TiNO is a gas generator that provides a tankless, direct, cost-effective, and unlimited supply of inhaled Nitric Oxide for the treatment of Pulmonary Hypertension and other respiratory conditions in children. TiNO is a device that can be universally connected to and calibrated with various pediatric ventilation systems to provide limitless Nitric Oxide to kids in need. Nitric Oxide is easily produced from the air in a cutting-edge ionization chamber and does not need to be stored in tanks.
