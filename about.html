My Story

To become a user experience designer in Atlanta where I can be creative, solve problems and create meaningful relationships, I want an employer that values diversity, encourages professional growth and can provide a healthy work/life balance. As a former visual merchandiser and manager for retail stores related to design, I've experienced firsthand how to present visually pleasing displays that effectively guide customers and create solutions in design for clients needing assistance. I have a desire to create experiences that are effective, enjoyable, and easy to navigate or understand. To achieve these experiences, I use my user testing, problem-solving and organizational skills, along with my eye for creativity. I am a dedicated and loyal individual who values a company that respects and values its employees.

Currently Based in Georgia, living in the the great city of Atlanta I spend my days with family and friends. I enjoy learning about new experiences and discovering hidden gems within the city I love. I studied at Georgia State University and earned my bachelor's in Fine Arts. Wanting to further my knowledge in design I completed a 12 month long program with CareerFoundry and gained a certification in UX (User Experience) Design. After earning my certification I continued to expand my knowledge of web design and learned basic principles in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

During my studies at Georgia State University I was fortunate enough to partake in a study abroad program based in Florence, Italy for a spring semester. I was able to travel across Europe to different countries and immerse myself in the history and culture. Growing up my family and I often spent summer vacations abroad, however, the independence I had as a young adult there gave me a new found love and appreciation for everything the world had to offer. I showed that appreciation through another passion of mine; photography.
